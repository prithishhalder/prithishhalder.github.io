About Me

PhD in Dust in Astrophysics (Assam University). Researcher in cosmic dust, light scattering, solar physics, and atmospheric microplastics. Developer of JaSTA and REST. Awarded Marie Curie Seal of Excellence (2025).

Research Interests

  • Cosmic Dust: Comet, Asteroid, Interstellar & Circum-galactic dust
  • Light Scattering: Polarization, Absorption, Extinction (Mie, T-matrix, DDA)
  • Solar Physics: Magnetic topology, SEPs, Solar wind
  • Atmospheric Science: Black carbon, Airborne microplastics
  • Nanoscience: Perovskites, Nanocrystals, Plasmonics
